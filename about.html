Why we started

Aurum Drive was founded in 2016 by two automotive enthusiasts who kept encountering the same frustration: every rental company treated cars as interchangeable white goods. Scratched interiors, inconsistent service, vehicles that smelled of the last passenger's drive.

We believed there was a market for something different - a company where the condition of each vehicle was a point of pride, not a cost to manage. Where the booking process was as smooth as the drive itself.

Ten years later, we operate across 18 cities with a fleet of over 200 vehicles. The founding belief hasn't changed.

What drives us

Our commitments

Uncompromised condition

Every vehicle in our fleet is inspected before and after each rental. If it doesn't pass our 42-point check, it doesn't leave the lot.

Transparent pricing

The price you see is the price you pay. Insurance, breakdown cover, and unlimited mileage are included in every booking. No hidden surcharges.

Your schedule, not ours

We operate around your itinerary. Airport collection at 3 am, hotel delivery on a Sunday - our team is available whenever you need them.

Responsible fleet

Forty percent of our fleet is now electric or hybrid. We're targeting a fully zero-emission lineup by 2030, without compromising on the cars you want to drive.
